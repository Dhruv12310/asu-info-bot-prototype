# Academic Advising

## Overview
Academic advising helps students plan coursework, understand degree requirements, and stay on track for graduation.

## Contact Information
- Website: https://students.asu.edu/advising

## Office Hours
Office hours vary by college and department.

## Common Requests
- Degree planning
- Course selection
- Graduation requirements
- Academic probation guidance

## Notes
Students should contact their specific college advising office for personalized advising.
