# Campus Safety

## Overview
Arizona State University provides campus safety services through ASU Police and emergency management resources.

## Contact Information
- Emergency: 911
- ASU Police (Non-Emergency): 480-965-3456
- Website: https://cfo.asu.edu/emergency

## Office Hours
Emergency services are available 24/7.

## Common Requests
- Emergency assistance
- Safety escorts
- Incident reporting
- Campus alerts

## Notes
Students should call 911 for emergencies requiring immediate assistance.
