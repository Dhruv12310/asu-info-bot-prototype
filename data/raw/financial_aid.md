# Financial Aid Office

## Overview
The Financial Aid Office at Arizona State University assists students with scholarships, grants, loans, and financial aid eligibility.

## Contact Information
- Email: financialaid@asu.edu
- Phone: 855-278-5080
- Website: https://students.asu.edu/financialaid

## Office Hours
- Monday–Friday: 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM (Arizona Time)

## Common Requests
- Financial aid application status
- FAFSA submission and corrections
- Scholarship eligibility
- Loan counseling and disbursement
- Verification requirements

## Notes
Students should use official ASU portals for submitting financial aid documents.
