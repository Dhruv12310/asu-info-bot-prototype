# Dean of Students Office

## Overview
The Dean of Students Office supports student advocacy, wellbeing, and academic integrity at Arizona State University.

- Phone: 480-965-6547
- Website: https://students.asu.edu/deanofstudents

## Common Requests
- Student advocacy and support
- Academic integrity concerns
- Student conduct questions
- Emergency assistance referrals

## Notes
The Dean of Students Office does not provide academic advising.
